It’s Nice to Hear You Ding

Wanna have a good sleeping
Wanna stay up late reading
But there’s too much work lumping
Nowhere complaining
Vacation is extending
Day and day do roll calling
Teacher’s doing name checking
While I’m giggling
It’s nice to hear you ding
Like midnight scary ring
Just like DAMA’s crazy dacing while I am hard working
It’s nice to hear you ding
Like the sound of wall scratching
Just like I am walking facing to the mud splashing
It’s nice to hear you ding
Like the ten tortures of QING
And I am like a poet going to argue with Viking
It’s nice to hear you ding
Like TANGCENG is chanting
Like I’m buried by “lost rivers” and old song annoying
It’s nice to hear you ding
Like do skr no CD
Like seeing a moving with a tittle “Director GuoJingming”
It’s nice to hear you ding
Like in dark we see Shining
We all messed up as it bothers get call phone shutting
Let us give a five-star praising in five times giving
